About Us

We specialise in bespoke digital platforms and applications design, engineering, integration and support for financial industry leaders across the UK, Switzerland, the EU and the US.

Our 150 people strong group has a stellar 25 year track record in delivering new digital products development and legacy modernisation services for financial industry clients in the UK, Switzerland, the US and a number of other countries across Western and Northern Europe.

Our customers include leading insurers, banks, investment managers, financial information providers and a top 3 stock exchange. We are also a technology partner for a number of fintech startups.

Our dedicated teams of digital solutions architects, software developers, UI designers, QA engineers, analysts and PMs offer a rare combination of deep understanding of the financial services domain and bespoke software engineering excellence.
